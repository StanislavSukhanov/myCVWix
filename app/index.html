my vision

To join WiX team on a full-time basis, to gain, improve and share knowledge. To develop a First-Class software, which users love, and I am proud of, using modern and advanced technologies.  

my story

My journey to programming has started in September 2017 with CS50 course. During this course I’ve started with C and gradually started to scratch a surface of web development: worked with Python NLTK to analyze sentiments of tweets via TwitterAPI, worked with SQLite database and Python Flask, worked with JQuery, AJAX and GoogleMaps API. As a final project I chose Python Django to create a "e-shop website".

After CS50 I’ve asked myself – what's next? I know, it's crazy (JS, HTML and CSS were the most challenging for me at that point of time), however I decided to go with Frontend Development, because I love beautiful things and the idea of creating “the face” of websites sounded and still sounds thrilling to me. I took HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery and React courses on CodeAcademy in Spring 2018. This summer I’ve attended Frontend Marathon at Wix office in Kyiv and had an opportunity to see how real professionals real WiX, who participated in this event actually work – it's amazing! professionals work. I want to work at WiX!
